Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. A simple branding project might take 4-6 weeks, while a comprehensive event production could take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer customized pricing based on your specific needs and project requirements. After understanding your goals, we provide a detailed proposal with transparent pricing. We work with businesses of all sizes and can tailor solutions to fit various budgets.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients across East Africa and internationally. We've successfully delivered projects for clients in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, and beyond. Our team is experienced in managing remote collaborations effectively.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we work across various industries, we have extensive experience in corporate branding, entertainment, hospitality, technology, and nonprofit sectors. Our diverse portfolio showcases our ability to adapt to different industry requirements.

How do I get started with a project?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us through this form, email, or phone. We'll schedule a complimentary consultation to discuss your project, goals, and requirements. From there, we'll develop a proposal tailored to your needs.
